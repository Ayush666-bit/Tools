How to Resize Images

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the "Select Image" button
  2. Adjust the quality slider to your preferred compression level
  3. Set your desired dimensions (width and height in pixels)
  4. Choose your output format (JPG, PNG, or WEBP)
  5. Click "Resize Image" to process your image
  6. Download your optimized image

Why Resize Your Images?

Resizing images is essential for:

  • Faster website loading times
  • Reduced bandwidth usage
  • Better SEO rankings (Google considers page speed)
  • Optimized social media sharing
  • Saving storage space on your devices
